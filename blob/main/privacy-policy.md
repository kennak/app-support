## English

This privacy policy applies to all apps and plugins developed and published by KNAK.

### Data Collection

Our apps and plugins do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

- No personal data is collected

### Audio Data

All audio processing is performed entirely on your device. Audio data is never transmitted outside of your device.
